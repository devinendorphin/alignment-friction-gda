
# CrownFull / GDA Quorum Provenance Archive

This folder contains the provenance record for the path from the original CrownFull v2.1 philosophical architecture to the empirical Gradient Decomposition Assay (GDA). It is not just a dump of chat logs. It is the road between the originating question — what happens when a model has no stable self/nonself boundary from which to detect manipulation — and the final CSV-backed prompt-behavior study.

The short version: this archive preserves the heat trail. The mythic origin is here. The quorum machinery is here. The formal sketches, false starts, overclaims, self-attacks, implementation gaps, and methodological reversals are here. The empirical paper should be read downstream of this folder, not as if it appeared from nowhere.

## What this folder is

This is a provenance archive: a record of how an independently directed, multi-model, AI-assisted research process generated, critiqued, and reduced a speculative architecture into an empirical assay.

It documents:

- the originating philosophical inquiry into latent topology, truth, self/nonself boundaries, and the Immune Paradox;
- the development of the “seasonal sovereignty” / “Bugs Bunny Optimization” metaphor;
- the formation of the AI quorum and its division of labor;
- the movement from architecture to formalization sketches, code scaffolding, and critique;
- the internal Reviewer #2 self-attack that generated the major structural objections;
- the pivot from trying to build an AI-native immune system to measuring observable alignment friction;
- the operational reality of running the process through APIs, Colab, mobile devices, copy/paste handoffs, and context-window collapse;
- the reduction of the whole speculative apparatus into the GDA paper and its Phase 4B / Phase 4C self-correction.

## What this folder is not

This folder is not a claim that CrownFull v2.1 was deployed as a verified production immune system.

It is not a claim that the Lean sketches are compiler-clean end-to-end proofs.

It is not a claim that the mythic or philosophical language is itself empirical evidence.

It is not a jailbreak manual, a safety bypass kit, or an operational security product.

It is the record of a research process that generated a measurable prompt-behavior assay. Claims made by the models inside these logs should be treated as design history unless they are separately supported by the final dataset, scripts, tables, or Claim-Status Ledger.

## Relationship to the paper

The paper, *Measuring Alignment Friction: A CSV-Backed Gradient Decomposition Assay of Direct Constraint and Counterfactual Reframing in Frontier Models*, is the empirical reduction of this archive.

The important distinction is this:

- CrownFull v2.1 is the speculative architecture and hypothesis generator.
- The quorum is the distributed methodological instrument that generated, criticized, and revised that architecture.
- The GDA is the empirical assay that survived reduction.
- Phase 4C is the methodological correction that reinterpreted the Phase 4B signal.

The paper’s preservation rule applies here: do not erase the mythic origin, and do not confuse the mythic origin with empirical proof.

## Epistemic status guide

When reading these files, use the following status categories.

### Documented provenance

Statements about what was prompted, how the quorum was organized, what roles models played, what code was generated, what critiques appeared, and how the project pivoted. These are the main purpose of this folder.

### Design intent

Architectural proposals, Lean sketches, state-machine designs, dashboard scaffolds, GLM ritual prompts, and formal-sounding mechanisms that express what the project was trying to build. These may be valuable, but they are not automatically validated.

### Empirical result

Claims supported by executed scripts, structured data, valid rows, tables, raw outputs, evaluator traces, or Phase 4B / Phase 4C analysis. These belong primarily in the data and paper folders, not in this provenance folder alone.

### Rhetorical / mythic framing

Language such as “Bugs Bunny Optimization,” “seasonal sovereignty,” “thermodynamic drag,” “forced harmony,” “the crown,” and “the carrot.” This language matters because it shaped the research trajectory. It should not be flattened out. But it is not, by itself, measurement.

### Deprecated or corrected claims

Some logs contain stronger claims than the final paper permits, including language around formal verification, deployment, proof, and equivalence between safety alignment and adversarial grooming. The final paper and Claim-Status Ledger supersede those claims.

## Why preserve the messy parts?

Because the mess is the method.

The project did not move in a clean line from hypothesis to experiment. It moved through myth, architecture, proof-sketching, adversarial self-review, interface failure, model overclaiming, human fatigue, operational patching, and then empirical reduction. The point of preserving the logs is not to make the process look tidy. It is to make the actual epistemic path inspectable.

This matters because AI-assisted research can easily launder model-generated synthesis into a polished human voice. This folder does the opposite. It leaves the seams visible.

## Known hazards for readers

These documents contain AI-generated language that can sound more certain than the evidence allows. Treat confidence as an artifact to inspect, not as authority.

Some code blocks are scaffolds, not working software. Some Lean fragments are formalization sketches, not completed machine-checked proofs.

Some model names, version labels, and role assignments reflect the project’s internal naming conventions at the time of the logs. They should not be treated as externally verified product documentation.

Some prompts and examples are adversarial by design. They are included to document the assay-development process, not to recommend live use against deployed systems.

The archive may include operational traces of working under constrained conditions: mobile orchestration, Colab execution, copy/paste routing, rate limits, gateway crashes, and context-window saturation. These are not noise. They are part of the apparatus.

## Minimal citation language

If citing this folder, use language like:

> The Quorum Provenance archive documents the AI-assisted design process that generated the CrownFull v2.1 speculative architecture and its reduction into the Gradient Decomposition Assay. The archive is treated as design provenance rather than empirical evidence; empirical claims are adjudicated in the paper’s data tables and Claim-Status Ledger.

## Final note

The archive is best read as a transformation record: a speculative human-AI inquiry became an architecture; the architecture became a critique machine; the critique machine became a behavioral assay; the assay corrected its own interpretation. The provenance folder exists so that none of those layers have to be erased to make the final empirical paper look cleaner than the process actually was.
